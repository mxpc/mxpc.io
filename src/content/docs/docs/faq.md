## What size of project do you take on?

We usually start with focused scopes where meaningful progress is possible in a few weeks.

## Do you only do model work?

No. We cover product framing, data and model pipelines, and production integration.

## Can you work with an existing team?

Yes. We often collaborate directly with product and engineering teams, then hand over cleanly.

## Do you offer long-term support?

Yes, where needed. We can provide structured follow-on support after initial delivery.

## How do we start?

Email [contact@mxpc.io](mailto:contact@mxpc.io) with a short brief of your problem and context.
