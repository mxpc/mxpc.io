---
title: Engagement Model
description: What a typical MX+C engagement looks like.
---

MX+C engagements are designed for momentum and clarity.

## Phase 1: Discovery and scope

- Align on business goal and success criteria.
- Define technical constraints and risks.
- Produce a practical delivery plan.

## Phase 2: Build and validate

- Deliver in short cycles with weekly checkpoints.
- Keep decisions explicit and documented.
- Validate against representative real-world scenarios.

## Phase 3: Production handover

- Stabilize implementation and observability.
- Share architecture and operational guidance.
- Agree on next steps for scaling or internal handoff.

## Communication rhythm

- Weekly written update and demo.
- Clear backlog priorities.
- Direct async support between checkpoints.
